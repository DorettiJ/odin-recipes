Gabagool Sandwich

Description

We call this the Gabagool as an ode to James Gandolfini and The Sopranos

  • 4 hoagie buns
  • 4 oz of mayo
  • 1/2 head romain lettuce
  • 2 oz italian dressing
  • 1 pound of Gabagool
  • 4 oz of pickled banana peppers
  • *OPTIONAL* 8 slices of American cheese
  • 4 oz of company sauce

Instructions

  1. Cut buns in half
  2. Spread that mayo
  3. lettuce + italian dressing + buns = goold time
  4. add the gabagool to a skillet; cook for 5 minutes or until crispy
  5. add peppers and cheee to gool on the skillet
  6. cook until cheese is melted
  7. gool+peppers+cheese+company sauce +lettuce buns = sammy
