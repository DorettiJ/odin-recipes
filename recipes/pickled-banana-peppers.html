Pickled Banana Peppers

Description

BANANA

  • 6 banana peppers
  • 12 oz white vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 bay leaf

Instructions

  1. Cut peppers 1/8-inch thick
  2. add all other ingreedients to pot
  3. boil until salt & sugar are disolved
  4. let it cool to room temp
  5. pour over peppers and let them sit for 3 hours
